What it does today

Four functions, one agent

01 · Forecast
Demand prediction
Blends historical sales velocity with seasonality and market signals to project demand 7–30 days out per SKU.
02 · Monitor
Inventory risk tracking
Flags products approaching stock-out or overstock thresholds before they become a problem on the floor.
03 · Evaluate
Supplier risk scoring
Scores suppliers on on-time delivery, defect rate, and lead-time variance, and recommends alternates when risk rises.
04 · Act
Auto-generated purchase orders
Drafts purchase orders and inventory reports at recommended quantities, ready for a manager to approve.
05 · Converse
Natural language Q&A
Managers ask plain questions — "what should I order next month" — and get a direct, data-backed answer.
06 · Explain
Reasoned recommendations
Every suggestion comes with the "why" — the data points behind it — so decisions stay auditable, not opaque.
